About us

We are a small shop located in Maple Valley, WA. Started back in 1999, producing the highest quality rods on the market has been our number one priority. We use the highest quality parts to ensure that your fishing experiences go as smoothly as possible and increase your catch rate! You can count on our rods for having the best sensitivity, being lightweight and having enough backbone to land those large fall Chinooks!
